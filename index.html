Welcome!

This is a website for the music of Gabriel Mukobi. Gabe is a young musician currently studying at Camas High School in Camas, WA. Musically, Gabe is a multi-instrumentalist, singer, songwriter, producer, audio engineer, businessman, and, most importantly, learner.

On this site, you can find:

  • Information about the nature of and where to get Gabe's music
  • A description of the groups with which Gabe has had the pleasure to perform
  • A breakdown of the wide range of instruments and gear that Gabe plays and uses
  • A gallery of photos, videos, and other media concerning Gabe and his music
  • Information regarding lessons with Gabe
  • How to contact Gabe

As of now, this website is very much a work in progress, and many things will be added, removed, changed, fixed, combined, separated, etc. very soon. If you see anything that seems wrong, Gabe would much appreciate it if you could let him know.

Please feel free to look around, enjoy your stay, and share this website with your friends if you like it. Thank you for visiting!
