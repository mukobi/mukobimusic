Mukobi Music


by Gabriel Mukobi

Welcome!

Hello! I am Gabriel Mukobi, and this is a website for my music. I am a young musician currently studying at Camas High School in Camas, WA. Musically, I am a multi-instrumentalist, singer, songwriter, producer, audio engineer, businessman, and, most importantly, learner.

On this site, you can find:

  • Information about the nature of and where to get my music.
  • A description of the groups with which I have had the pleasure to perform.
  • A breakdown of the wide range of instruments and gear that I play and use.
  • A gallery of photos, videos, and other media concerning me and my music.
  • Information regarding lessons with me.
  • How to contact me.

As of now, this website is very much a work in progress, and many things will be added, removed, changed, fixed, combined, separated, etc. very soon. If you see anything that seems wrong, I would much appreciate it if you could let me know.

Please feel free to look around, enjoy your stay, and share this website with your friends if you like it. Thank you for visiting!

Latest News

Presently, this website, my music career, and I are still very young. I am in the procees of writing, recording, and producing a whole lot of cool music which I intend to release in a full album before the end of this year (2017). Please check back in for news regarding that.

Very soon, you can expect some of the following things to be added to this website:

  • Pictures and videos of me performing.
  • Discussions about my musical mindset, songwriting approach, influences, etc.
  • Samples of new music I am working on that I would very much appreciate your opinions on.

And as I start to wrap up my debut album:

  • Surveys asking you to help rank my music, determine titles, etc.
  • News about any big leaps forward in the creation and/or release of the album.
  • Links to listen to, stream, and download the album.
